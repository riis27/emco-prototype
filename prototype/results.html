Inflamed acne → calm & clear

Service: Acne Bootcamp + Hydrafacial boosts
Products: Face Reality routine, ingredient cleanup

This client came in with inflamed breakouts, sensitivity, and a shelf full of well-meaning but pore-clogging products. Together, we built a structured Acne Bootcamp plan using a customized Face Reality regimen, strategic lifestyle shifts, and occasional Hydrafacial treatments for congestion.

By month six, their inflammation had calmed, new breakouts slowed significantly, and their barrier finally felt comfortable instead of tight and sore.

Texture & congestion → smoother glow

Service: Hydrafacial series + maintenance facials
Products: Acne-safe exfoliants, barrier support moisturizers

This client struggled with tiny bumps, blackheads, and a stubborn dullness that makeup couldn’t hide. We started with a series of customized Hydrafacial treatments to deeply cleanse pores, then rotated in results-driven facials for pigment and texture.

At home, we simplified their routine with a gentle acne-safe exfoliant and hydrating barrier support, avoiding heavy occlusives flagged by the Ingredient Checker. Over a few months, the texture smoothed, pores looked more refined, and the natural glow came back.

Barrier repair & redness reduction

Service: Barrier Repair Facial
Products: Gentle cleanser, barrier serum, SPF

This client had tried to “fix” their skin with harsh scrubs and over-exfoliation, leaving them red, reactive, and uncomfortable. We paused active treatments and focused on a dedicated barrier repair facial series plus a soothing, minimal home routine.

After several weeks of consistent barrier support and gentle treatments, the redness softened, flaking reduced, and their skin could finally tolerate more targeted acne or pigment work again—without freaking out.
