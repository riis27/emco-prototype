What is Hydrafacial?

Hydrafacial uses a vortex tip to gently exfoliate, infuse a light peel, perform painless extractions, and bathe skin in hydrating serums.

Treatment steps

  1. Cleanse + exfoliate
  2. Gentle peel
  3. Vacuum extractions
  4. Hydration + antioxidant infusion

Best for

  • Congestion and blackheads
  • Pre-event glow
